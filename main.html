Projectile Motion

Motion of a particle launched with speed u at angle θ in a uniform gravitational field g. Air resistance ignored.

Range R ≈ – m
Max height H – m
Flight time T – s

Simulation View

Axis: x (horizontal), y (vertical)

The path is a parabola: y = x tanθ − (g x²) / (2 u² cos²θ), derived from x = u cosθ · t and y = u sinθ · t − ½ g t².
